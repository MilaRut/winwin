FAQ

Who can participate in the campaign?

All WINWIN Partners affiliates who have a personal account on the affiliate programme website and have fulfilled the campaign conditions can participate.

Campaign period

The campaign period runs from 01.05.2026 to 30.06.2026.

What conditions must be met to get a chance to win the main prize?

Register on the affiliate programme website and collect a minimum of 200 points during the campaign period. If you already have a personal account, no additional registration is required.

Which brands are participating in the campaign?

One brand is participating in the campaign: WinWinBet.

Which GEOs are participating in the campaign?

All GEOs available on the WinWinBet brand are participating in the campaign.

How are points credited?

1 confirmed FTD = 1 point $100 in new deposits = 1 point

Which deposits are counted?

All confirmed player deposits (FTDs) during the campaign period are counted. The total amount of deposits during the campaign period must be at least $10,000.

Which traffic sources are prohibited?

Prohibited sources include: incentivised traffic, SMS mailings, brand traffic, and other types of traffic not approved by the manager.

Is verification required to participate?

Verification is not required to participate in the campaign. Passing KYC may be required to receive prizes or payouts.

How often is the leaderboard updated?

The leaderboard is updated three times a week — on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays.

What prizes are provided in the campaign?

The winners are determined by the leaderboard results based on the number of points collected.
●1st place – the affiliate receives a prize or a cash payout in the equivalent amount of $10,000
●2nd place – the affiliate receives a prize or a cash payout in the equivalent amount of $5,000
●3rd place – the affiliate receives a prize or a cash payout in the equivalent amount of $3,000
●4th to 10th places – affiliates receive a prize or a cash payout in the equivalent amount of $1,000
●11th to 20th places – affiliates receive a cash payout of $300
●21st to 50th places – affiliates receive a cash payout of $200

When are the results summarised and when are the prizes awarded?

The results of the campaign will be summarised on the 3rd of July 2026. The final leaderboard will be updated and published on the campaign landing page on 03.07.2026. A mandatory condition for all participants is achieving a total deposit volume of at least $10,000 for the entire campaign period.
Participants who fail to meet this condition will not be included in the final ranking. The crediting and issuing of prizes will be carried out within 14 days after the results have been summarised.

Who should I contact regarding campaign questions?

For all questions related to the campaign, please contact your affiliate manager.
